# The World Model: Portfolio Website Framework

## Overview
A four-layer methodology for building a portfolio with AI while maintaining human control and clarity.

---

## Layer 1: Discovery
**Establishing definitions and constraints**

Before designing anything, we defined five formal principles that every design decision must satisfy:

1. **Minimalist** - Every element serves the narrative. Intentional whitespace. Typography restraint. If it doesn't serve the story, it's deleted.

2. **ClearNarrative** - Opens with self → explains philosophy → logical flow. The reader follows easily. No skipping, no confusion.

3. **TextEmphasis** - Writing-driven. Explains methodology, not just tools. Authentic language. Shows thinking.

4. **AuthenticVoice** - Personal, vulnerable, conversational. Tool-transparent. Sounds like a real person, not corporate copy.

5. **ProcessTransparency** - Shows methodology, constraints, iteration. Discloses AI use. Nothing hidden.

---

## Layer 2: Translation
**Decision logic and priorities**

Formula: `ClearNarrative(choice) ≻ AuthenticVoice(choice) ∧ Constraint Intersection → Distinctiveness`

**Priority order:**
1. **Clarity** - The reader must understand
2. **Distinctiveness** - This must feel unique to this person
3. **Authenticity** - It must sound real
4. **Transparency** - Show the work

---

## Layer 3: Execution
**Building within constraints**

Design system:
- **Colors:** Teal (#2B9B9E) = systems/AI, Purple (#7C3AED) = creativity/human
- **Typography:** Sans-serif (bold) for headlines, system sans for body
- **Layout:** Mobile-first, full-width responsive
- **Animations:** Smooth fade-ins, scroll-triggered, no bounce

Projects:
- Iron Swords (crisis coordination system)
- Portfolio Website (this site itself)
- Book Distribution (infrastructure & relationships)

---

## Layer 4: Feedback Loop
**Continuous refinement**

8 approval cycles (vs. 47 without framework):
- Each decision tested against definitions
- Clear feedback mechanism
- Iterative, intentional improvements
- Human maintained control throughout

---

## The Lesson

Instead of guessing what you want, frame the problem formally. Then AI can work *within* your constraints, not around them.
